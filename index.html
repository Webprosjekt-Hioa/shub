So what can it do?

Student hub collects all your information into one place

Studenthub will gather data from several information channels. It will process the data and prioritize based on due date, importance and the user’s customizations.

You will be notified when new information is given, when a deadline is approaching and when you receive new student e-mail. Studenthub will also tell you when a paper is graded or when a new assignment is given.

The information will be displayed in an intuitive way optimized for both computers and mobile units using responsive web design. You will be able to efficiently get a overview of your school information and progress.
