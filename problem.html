Our solution

This is the current situation

University is full of information and different information channels. In HIOA we have a total of seven different information channels. This is too many information channels to keep track of, and it’s here the problem starts. When you get your obligatory tasks in one site, feedback on your essays on a student mail, and the time table on a different site again, it’s starts to get confusing. We have asked over a hundred students, and the fact is, we need change now.

Our research

We made a survey consisting of five questions. The survey was conducted in November 2017 among 100 different HIOA students, from different areas of study. We explained that this survey was made to back up our claims about the HIOA’s information channels was too many, and that it didn’t work as good as it should. Here are our results:

Here we see the results of witch year the different students were attending. The idea about this question, is to map out how many students form each year that was attending the survey. As you can see, it was a big group of students from year one to three, but a minimal group of master students.

This bar shows witch of the different information channels the students are using. We can see that the three main information channels are Fronter, stud.hioa.no and facebook. Almost every student uses every single one of the information channels, but as you can see on the pie chart below, most of the students think that it’s ineffective with these many channels of information.

This pie chart shows how many of the students who think it’s hard to find necessary information about due dates, obligatory tasks and status on the essays handed in. As we can see from this pie chart, most of the students think that it’s hard to find the information they need.

As this pie chart shows, many of the students miss important information that they need to know about obligatory tasks, classroom changes and more because of the many information channels.

This pie chart shows that almost all the students would use a website that gathered all the information in one site if this solution was provided too them. This shows that it’s a big need to make a solution to this major problem.

The conclusion

The conclusion that we can make from all this information, is that we need something new. We need a solution that gathers all the information the students need, so we can make life easier for the students, and the teachers.
